What is Khub?

Khub is the official Knowledge Hub of the Philippine Science High School system. It is where most of the student's learning materials is posted, as well as where most requirements were submitted during the online setup during 2020-2022. Now that we're back to face-to-face classes, it's become an archive for teachers' slides for student to reread once more and for some submissions, such as videos, are passed.

You only gain access to your courses once a teacher enrolls you in one. Some subject courses are specific to only your section, some are available to the entire batch.
